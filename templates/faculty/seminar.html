Best Practices for Seminars
Before the Seminar:
  • • Give ample notice to students (2+ weeks)
  • • Promote through posters and announcements
  • • Arrange venue and technical setup
  • • Brief the speaker about audience
  • • Send reminder notifications
During & After the Seminar:
  • • Start on time with proper introduction
  • • Allow Q&A session
  • • Facilitate networking opportunities
  • • Take photos and feedback
  • • Thank the speaker formally
